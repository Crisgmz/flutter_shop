8) ANIMACIONES Y TRANSICIONES
================================================================================

Keyframes definidos:
  fade-in:       opacity 0→1, translateY 8px→0, 0.3s ease-out
  slide-in-left: translateX -100%→0, 0.3s ease-out
  accordion-down: height 0→auto, 0.2s ease-out
  accordion-up:   height auto→0, 0.2s ease-out

Uso:
  - KPICard, DataTableShell, Cards: clase "animate-fade-in"
  - Sidebar: "slide-in-left" (implícito por shadcn)
  - Hover en cards: "transition-colors" (150ms default Tailwind)
  - Hover border: "hover:border-primary/50"
  - Cursor: "cursor-pointer" en cards clickeables
